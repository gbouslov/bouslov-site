Day-by-Day Itinerary
DAY 1
Fri, May 15
Fly to Barbados
ORD → BGI, American AA2984, 5:00 AM → 3:32 PM (1 stop MIA). Arrive mid-afternoon. Check into Beach Villas on Worthing Beach. Walk to a beach bar, rum punch, feet in the sand. You're here.
DAY 2
Sat, May 16
South Coast & East Coast
Morning: Surf or swim at Worthing Beach (steps from your door). Carlisle Bay shipwrecks — snorkel over 6 sunken ships in 15–40 ft of crystal water. Sea turtles, rays, tropical fish. Gear rental on the beach ~$15.
Afternoon: Drive to Bathsheba on the wild Atlantic coast. Soup Bowl surf break, dramatic rock formations, tidal pools. Harrison's Cave — underground limestone caverns (~$30/pp).
Evening: Dinner in Holetown — west coast restaurant strip.
DAY 3
Sun, May 17
Catamaran Day Cruise
Full day: West coast catamaran cruise — sail along the platinum coast, multiple snorkel stops over reefs, swim with turtles, lunch & unlimited rum punch on board. Book "Cool Runnings IV" or "Good Times" catamaran (~$80–100/pp).
Alt: Folkestone Marine Park reef snorkeling + Animal Flower Cave at the northern tip (sea caves with natural pools).
DAY 4
Mon, May 18
Fly to St. Vincent
Check out of Airbnb. Taxi to BGI airport (~$25).
BGI → SVD · Caribbean Airlines BW223 · 12:35 PM → 1:20 PM · 45 min nonstop · Conf: B9O48V
Pick up rental car at SVD airport ($55/day). Drive to your Airbnb. Explore Kingstown or hit a beach. Get groceries for the week.
Note: You'll need a temporary SVG driving permit (~$65 total, get at airport or police station).
DAY 5
Tue, May 19
La Soufriere Volcano Hike
Full day: Hike to the crater of an active volcano (4,049 ft). 2-hour climb through cloud forest to the rim. Last erupted 2021. Misty, dramatic, unforgettable. Book a local guide (~$40–50/pp). Start from Bamboo Range or Rabacca trailhead. Leave early — clearest skies are morning.
Evening: You'll be tired. Cook at the Airbnb, watch the sunset, early bed.
DAY 6
Wed, May 20
Waterfalls & Dark View Falls
Morning: Trinity Falls — triple waterfall in the rainforest. Shorter hike than the volcano, great swimming hole at the base.
Afternoon: Dark View Falls — twin waterfalls via suspension bridge. Easy access, swim under the falls.
Detour: Vermont Nature Trail — birdwatching, spot the St. Vincent Parrot (national bird, endemic).
DAY 7
Thu, May 21
Bequia Day Trip
Morning: Drive to Kingstown ferry terminal. 1-hour ferry to Bequia (~$5 each way). Walk to Princess Margaret Beach — one of the best in the Caribbean.
Afternoon: Old Hegg Turtle Sanctuary. Explore Port Elizabeth harbor town. Lunch at a waterfront spot — fresh catch, cheap rum.
Evening: Ferry back to St. Vincent. Or overnight in Bequia if you want more time.
DAY 8
Fri, May 22
Tobago Cays — The Highlight
Full day sailing trip: This is the reason you came. Uninhabited islands in a marine park. Swim with green sea turtles in the clearest water you've ever seen. Snorkel Horseshoe Reef. Grilled lobster lunch on the beach. Book Friendship Rose schooner or a local charter from Bequia or Union Island (~$100–150/pp including lunch).
Alt: If departing from St. Vincent mainland, book a power boat charter to the Cays (~$200–300 for the boat, split between groups).
DAY 9
Sat, May 23
