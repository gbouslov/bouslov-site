DAY 2
Fri, May 15
South Coast & Surfing
Morning: Surf or bodyboard at Worthing Beach (right outside your door) or Rockley Beach (5 min walk)
Afternoon: Snorkel at Carlisle Bay shipwrecks — 6 sunken ships in shallow clear water, swim with sea turtles (20 min drive)
Evening: Oistins Fish Fry — the legendary Friday night street food market. Fresh grilled fish, rum punch, live music.
DAY 3
Sat, May 16
East Coast Adventure
Morning: Drive to Bathsheba — dramatic Atlantic coast, the famous Soup Bowl surf break. Watch or rent a board.
Afternoon: Harrison's Cave — underground limestone caverns with stalactites, waterfall, crystalline pools
Detour: Welchman Hall Gully — hike through a collapsed cave turned tropical forest (30 min trail)
DAY 4
Sun, May 17
West Coast & Catamaran
Full day: Catamaran cruise down the west coast — snorkel stops over reefs, swim with turtles, lunch on board, rum punch included. Book "Cool Runnings" or "Good Times" catamaran.
Alt: Folkestone Marine Park for reef snorkeling + Animal Flower Cave (sea caves at the island's north tip)

DAYS 6–7
Tue–Wed
May 19–20
St. Vincent — Volcano & Waterfalls
La Soufriere Volcano: 2-hour hike to the crater rim of an active volcano (4,049 ft). Misty, dramatic, unforgettable. Book a guide — the trail starts from Bamboo Range or Rabacca. Full day.
Trinity Falls: Triple waterfall deep in the rainforest — shorter hike, great swimming hole at the base.
Dark View Falls: Twin waterfalls accessible via suspension bridge. Easy hike, swim under the falls.
Vermont Nature Trail: Birdwatching through rainforest — spot the St. Vincent Parrot (national bird).
DAYS 8–10
Thu–Sat
May 21–23
The Grenadines — Tobago Cays & Island Hopping
This is the highlight of the trip. Take a multi-day sailing trip or day trips from Bequia/Union Island:

Tobago Cays: Uninhabited islands in a marine park. Crystal clear water, swim with green sea turtles, snorkel Horseshoe Reef, eat grilled lobster on the beach. Best snorkeling in the Caribbean.
Bequia: Take the ferry from Kingstown (1 hour). Laid-back island, great walks, Old Hegg Turtle Sanctuary, Princess Margaret Beach.
Mustique: Ultra-exclusive island (day trip from Bequia). Beautiful hiking trails, Basil's Bar.
Mayreau: Tiny island (300 people), Salt Whistle Bay — one of the most beautiful beaches in the world.
Kitesurfing: Union Island is one of the Caribbean's top kite spots.
