About the ports

About the ports

Antigua — Nelson's Dockyard (UNESCO), Shirley Heights lookout, 365 beaches. English Harbour is stunning.
St. Kitts — Brimstone Hill Fortress (UNESCO), scenic railway circling the island, rainforest zip-lining, quiet beaches. Basseterre has great colonial architecture. Nevis (sister island) is a short ferry ride — even more peaceful.
